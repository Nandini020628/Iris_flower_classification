About Iris Flower Species

Iris Setosa

Iris setosa is a small, blue-violet flower found in northern wetlands. It has short petals and wide leaves.

Iris Versicolor

Also known as the Blue Flag Iris, it grows in marshes and has striking violet-blue petals with yellow markings.

Iris Virginica

This species features elegant purple petals and thrives in wet meadows and along streams in the U.S. southeast.
